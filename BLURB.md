# Short blurb (social / repo header)

Olfactory Constellation is a free, open-source tool that maps molecular smell data two ways —
by chemical structure and by odor — and automatically spots "activity cliffs," where nearly
identical molecules smell completely different. It brings structure, vibrational-spectra
clustering, odor, and chemical identity into one explorable view, built to help investigate an
old open question: how molecular structure and vibration relate to what we actually smell. It's
an instrument for exploration, not a claim about how smell works — point it at public data and
see the patterns for yourself.

— Rick Scianablo Jr · MIT License · 2026
